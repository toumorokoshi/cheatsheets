Russian Alphabet (Cyrillic)
===========================
====== =====================
Letter Pronunciation
------ ---------------------
А а    a, ah (cAr, Alligator)
Б б    b (Bit Bat)
В в    v (Vine, Vet, Voice)
Г г    g (Go, Guard, Good)
Д д    d (Do, Dog, Double)
Е е    hard y (Yet, Yes)
Ё ё    yo (York, Yolk, Yonder)
Ж ж    h (meaSure, pleaSure)
З з    z (Zoo, Zodiac)
И и    ee (mE, sEE, mEEt)
Й й    soft y (boY, oYster, Yes)
К к    k (Kitten, Kite)
Л л    l (Lamp, Lock)
М м    m (Map, My, Moon)
Н н    n (Not, Nose, Nine)
О о    o (mOre, AUdio)
П п    p (Pet, Pot, Point)
Р р    r (thRiller, rolling the r)
С с    s (See, Sun, Soft)
Т т    t (Tip, Task, Time)
У у    oo (bOOk, bOOth)
Ф ф    f (Face, Fact, Force)
Х х    (loch with scottish accent)
Ц ц    ts (siTS, thaT'S)
Ч ч    ch (CHip, CHair)
Ш ш    sh (SHip, SHut, SHe)
Щ щ    sh + ch (freSH CHicken)
Ы ы    kind of like 'uee'
Ы ы    s (See, Sun, Soft)
Э э    eh (mEt, bEt)
Ю ю    yu (YOU, Use)
Я я    ya/yuh (YArd, YUmmy)
Ь      adds a little 'yuh' (softener)
Ъ      hardens
