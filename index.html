reStructuredText basics

  • ======= (before and after) a document title
  • ------- (before and after) a document subtitle
  • ======= a header
  • ------- a subheader
  • ~~~~~~~ a sub-subheader
  • paragraph: blank line before and after

lists

Enumerated lists can start off with a number or letter, followed by a period, right bracket, or sorrounded by brackets.
  • *: list results in a circle
  • -: list results in a hollow dot
  • +: list results in a square
